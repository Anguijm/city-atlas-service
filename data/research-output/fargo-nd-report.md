**Other Interesting Spots:**
1.  **Fargo Theatre:** An immaculately restored 1926 Art Deco movie palace located on Broadway. It’s not just a cinema; it's a living piece of history, complete with a massive glowing marquee, showing first-run independent films and hosting community events.
2.  **Downtown Fargo Street Fair Grounds:** While the fair itself is an annual event, the downtown streets that host it are the heart of the city. Walking this area, you can imagine it transformed into North Dakota's largest free outdoor festival, bustling with arts and food.
3.  **College GameDay Location:** In 2013 and 2014, ESPN's College GameDay broadcast live from downtown, a testament to the area's ability to host major national events and the passionate local sports culture. Exploring the blocks around Broadway gives you a feel for this central public square.

**Other Interesting Spots:**
