Highlights

Find out more:

Peruvian food doesn’t get better than this. Well, in London at least. This world-renowned restaurant is famed for its incredible flavour combinations and intriguing textures – not to mention their utterly divine, melt-in-your-mouth ceviche. Its sister restaurant, Lima Floral, has also received high praise – earning a rare five-star review from our critics. Now, you can enjoy three courses and a glass of prosecco from the carefully crafted set menu for just £29. Christmas gifts, sorted.

Starters

Tuna chifa ceviche – crispy wontons, ginger and xo sauce

Classic seabream ceviche – traditional tiger’s milk, sweet potato and canchita corn

Duck crudo – castus cream, pisco macerated elderberries

Cereal causa

Mains

18-hour braised suckling pig – huaacatay and cuzco corn

Seabass fillet sudado – red onions, heritage tomatoes and chillies

Clay-baked 4000m potatoes – eco dry potatoes, tubers, pachamanca sauce

Lamb rump – celeriac, Andean potatoes, ocopa sauce

Puddings

Alfajores – dulce de leche ice cream

Chocolate mousse – 75% cacao chuncho and eucalyptus syrup

Guava ice cream – chocolate crumble, aji panca syrup

Cherimoya parfait
