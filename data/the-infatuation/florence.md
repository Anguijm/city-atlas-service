The Best Restaurants In Florence

Classic trattorias, fantastic steak, and where to eat like a local.

CORAL SISK & GEORGETTE JUPE

AUGUST 13, 2025

Giotto Pizzeria
ITALIAN
SANTA MARIA NOVELLA

There’s plenty of pizza in town, but Giotto near Santa Maria Novella is a standout for the attention to detail in every single pie.

GEORGETTE JUPE

AUGUST 13, 2025

Locale
EXPERIMENTAL
CENTRO STORICO

With themed menus, frescos in a former noble family palace, and a bar with an open-air vertical garden, Locale is one of the more fun fine dining spots in all of Italy, not just Florence.

AUGUST 13, 2025

Ristorante Nugolo
ITALIAN
SANT’AMBROGIO

Ristorante Nugolo serves dishes that go beyond just the Tuscan norms, and feels like a farmers market inside a greenhouse.

AUGUST 13, 2025

Antico Ristoro Di Cambi
ITALIAN
OLTRARNO

This trattoria has been serving comfort food from the Tuscan culinary vault to tourists and locals in the Oltrarno since 1950.

Trattoria Sostanza
ITALIAN
SANTA MARIA NOVELLA

Trattoria Sostanza has a menu that hasn’t changed since it opened in 1869, and it's a place in Florence you have to try at least once.
